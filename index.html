Pound Cake

American, traditional - dessert course

This is a cake traditionally made from one pound each of: flour, sugar, butter, and eggs. These can be made in loaf pans or in bundt pans.

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  1. Heat oven to 350 F
  2. Cream butter
  3. Mix in sugar
  4. Add eggs, one at a time
  5. Add half of the dry ingredints
  6. Add half of the milk
  7. Add remaining dry
  8. Add remaining milk
